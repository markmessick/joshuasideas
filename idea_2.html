Idea #2: Provide Each Field Expert with a Monthly Dashboard of Personal KPIs

What if Field Experts knew exactly what their strengths and weaknesses were and could see their growth from month-to-month?

One of the hardest parts of being a Field Expert is the lack of immediate feedback on job performance. During my first two months in the field I was constantly asking myself if I was doing a good job, or if I was just wasting Joshua's time and money and causing a bunch of reservices. I honestly didn't know.

Something that would benefit me a lot (and I'm sure the rest of the field experts) would be to receive a report each month detailing various trends in my Quarterly services. Using the Pestroutes API I could pull data for each Field Expert and generate monthly reports showing the following statistics:

*The average amount of time spent on each Quarterly service

*The number of Quarterly services that turned into reservices

*The most common causes of those reservices

*The application rate of each product

*Any other KPIs that you think would be beneficial that Pestroutes collects data for
