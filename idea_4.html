Idea #4: Analyze Seasonal & Geographical Reservice Trends

What if you could predict which pests will pose the biggest problem in each neighboorhood...and proactively prevent them from ever turning into reservices?

I'm not actually sure how useful this one would be, but I figured I'd toss it in anyways, you know? Can't hurt.

Basically, this program would cross-check the months and months of reservice data you've already collected with the latitude/longitude of the invovled customers. We could then use the results to plot heatmaps to show us which neighborhoods are most heavily affected by wasps, which are most heavily affected by mice, etc.

Actually, maybe this would be more useful for the sales guys. If they knew that a certain nieghborhood tends to have a mouse problem every winter, they could adjust their door approaches accordingly. I don't know though. Just thinking out loud here.
